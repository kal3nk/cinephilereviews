About Me

Who is the reviewer?

Hi, I'm Seth also known as Taiyo on my Letterboxd. I'm a 20-year-old Communication and Multimedia Design student at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (AUAS).

Next to that I spend most of my time watching films, that's why I made this website, as I feel limited by Letterboxd's features. On this website I cross reference my reviews, but also add some features that I can't on my Letterboxd. Things like The Top 3 of the Month and this about me page.

My Favorite Directors of All Time

Next to enjoying individual films, I also have some favorite directors. My favorite directors include names like;

Edgar Wright: An English director with a very distinct style that prioritizes an interaction between the visuals and the audio.

Park Chan-wook: A South-Korean director that is a better version of Quentin Tarantino, and then some. He is also my favorite director of all time.

Hirokazu Kore-eda: A Japanese director that is known for his found-family stories that make you feel something like never before.
